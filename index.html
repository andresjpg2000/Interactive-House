This is a demo of our Energy Monitoring Web App

Check out our app open source github repository

This interactive 3D house allows you to simulate real-time energy production (solar) and consumption by toggling lights and appliances. The actions you take here generate consumption data, just like in a real home. Our Energy Monitoring Web App processes this data to provide insights, reports, and benefits to the end user.

The simulation generates a new energy consumption or production record every 10 seconds. Each record reflects 1 hour of activity in the simulated environment, allowing for fast-forwarded analysis.

Controls:

  • Light controller buttons: Turns on or off the lights/appliances in the scene.
  • Toggle Scene Lights: Toggles the lights in the scene.
  • Click and drag to rotate the view around the house
  • Scroll to zoom in and out
  • Right-click and drag to pan around
